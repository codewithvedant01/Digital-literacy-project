## Social Media Responsibility Checklist

&#x09;

|**Do's**|**Don'ts**|
|-|-|
|Audit your privacy settings regularly to control who sees your personal posts.	|Don't post illegal activities or content involving underage drinking/substance use.|
|Don't post illegal activities or content involving underage drinking/substance use.|Don't vent about professors, classmates, or previous employers publicly.|
|Share your achievements, such as Dean’s List honors, projects, or volunteer work.|Don't engage in digital arguments or "flame wars" that can be screenshotted.|
|Fact-check information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.|Don't share sensitive data, like your student ID number, home address, or birthdate.<br />|
|Google yourself occasionally to see what a recruiter or internship coordinator sees.|Don't use offensive language or post "jokes" that could be interpreted as harassment.|
