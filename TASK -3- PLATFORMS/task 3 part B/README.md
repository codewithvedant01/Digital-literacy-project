# **🌐 Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz**



###### This repository contains the structure and questions for a **Google Forms** quiz designed to test and improve digital literacy among students. The quiz covers essential topics like cybersecurity, Digital Footprint, Password Security and The Digital Divide.



#### 📝 Quiz Overview

\*   **Target Audience:** Batchmates / Students

\*   **Platform:** Google Forms

\*   **Total Questions:** 7

\*   **Goal:** To promote safe, responsible, and critical use of digital technologies.

\*   **Form Link:**  [Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz ](https://forms.gle/mZah9ept7KLATKzA6)



#### 🚀 How to Set Up

1\.  **Create a Form:** Go to \[Google Forms](https://forms.google.com) and start a "Blank" or "Quiz" form.

2\.  **Enable Quiz Mode:** Go to **Settings** > **Make this a quiz**.

3\.  **Input Questions:** Copy the questions provided in the project files into the form fields.

4\.  **Configure Points:**

&#x20;   \*   Assign **10 points** for each Multiple Choice question.

&#x20;   \*   Assign **10 points** for Short Answer questions (manual grading recommended).

&#x20;   \*   **Assign 10 points f**or Long Answer questions

5\.  **Distribute:** Click **Submit** to generate a link or QR code for your batchmates.

#### 

#### 📚 Topics Covered

\- **Cybersecurity:** Phishing, Password strength, and 2FA.

\- **Information Literacy:** Identifying "Fake News" and verifying sources.

\- **Privacy:** Managing digital footprints and understanding cookies.

\- **Etiquette \& Wellness:** Online behavior (Netiquette) and screen-time balance.



##### ⚙️ Recommended Settings

\*   **Collect Emails:** Enable this to send results back to participants.

\*   **Shuffle Question Order:** Found under **Settings > Presentation** to prevent copying.

\*   **Response Validation:** For short answers, use "Minimum character count" to ensure thoughtful responses.
